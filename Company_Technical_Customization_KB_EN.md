## 3. Structural & Aesthetic Options

### Structural Configurations
*   **Monoblock (1-Piece)**: Maximum structural integrity and lightest weight for track and street.
*   **2-Piece Forged**: Separated barrel and center for flexible offset and multi-color combinations.
*   **3-Piece Forged**: Maximum customization; inner barrel, outer lip, and center disc.

### Special Styles & Design Features
*   **Deep Lip (Deep Dish)**: Engineered for extreme stance and wide-body kits.
*   **Deep Concave / Ultra Concave**: High-curvature spoke designs for aggressive visual depth.
*   **Big Size Truck / Dually**: Heavy-duty forged wheels for F-150, RAM, Silverado, and Super Duty trucks (supporting high load ratings).
*   **Off-road Forged**: Beadlock-style and rugged reinforced designs for 4x4 vehicles.

### Finishing & Color Collection
*   **Standard Colors**: S (Silver), B (Black), G (Gold/Grey), W (White).
*   **Premium Finishes**: HS (Hyper Silver), HB (Hyper Black), MS (Machine Silver), MB (Machine Black), MG (Machine Grey).
*   **Luxury Options**: CHROME, VC (Vacuum Coating/Chrome), BLP (Black Lip Polish).
*   **Custom Textures**: Brushed (Clear/Tinted), Polished, Candy Colors, Two-tone finishes, CNC Engraving.
